PREAMBLE: THE DECLARATION OF DIGITAL SOVEREIGNTY
We, the Architects, Builders, and Citizens of the RGB Spectrum, in order to form a more perfect digital union, establish justice, insure domestic tranquility, and secure the blessings of ownership to ourselves and our posterity, do ordain and establish this Constitution for the Nation of ColorIsLive.
We reject the "Feudal Model" of the Web 2.0 era, where users are serfs on platforms owned by silent oligarchs. We proclaim that:
1.	Code is Law: The rules of physics are immutable and transparent.
2.	Ownership is Absolute: What a citizen creates or buys cannot be seized without due process.
3.	Identity is Sovereign: The digital self is an extension of the human will, distinct from physical constraints.
This document serves as the supreme contract between the Governor (The System) and the Governed (The Users).

ARTICLE I: THE STRUCTURE OF POWER
The governance of the Nation is tri-partite, designed to balance the speed of centralized execution with the stability of decentralized consensus.
SECTION 1: THE IMPERIAL CROWN (THE EXECUTIVE)
The supreme executive authority is vested in the Emperor (The White Key - #FFFFFF).
•	Clause A (The Benevolent Dictator): During the "Genesis Era" (Years 1-5), the Emperor holds the Root Keys necessary to deploy hotfixes, mint the initial currency supply, and veto malicious smart contract exploits. This centralization is a security feature, not a bug, ensuring the survival of the infant nation.
•	Clause B (The Genesis Council): The Emperor is advised by a cabinet of 7 Ministers (The Primes), representing the fundamental axes of the world (War, Trade, Justice, Infrastructure). Major protocol upgrades (Hard Forks) require a super-majority consensus (5/8) between the Emperor and the Council.
•	Clause C (The Dead Man’s Switch): Should the Imperial Key become inactive for 90 consecutive days, administrative control automatically devolves to the Council via a multi-signature smart contract protocol.
SECTION 2: THE CODE (THE LEGISLATIVE)
The Legislature is not a room of people, but a repository of logic.
•	Clause A (Immutability): The fundamental constants of the universe—specifically the 16,777,216 Land Cap and the 24-bit Coordinate System—are hard-coded. No Decree, Vote, or Imperial Order can alter these physical limits. This protects investors against inflation.
•	Clause B (Dynamic Parameters): Variable economic parameters (Tax Rates, Entropy Decay Speed) can be adjusted. These adjustments are proposed by the Executive but must be ratified by the Tier 3 Architects (The Senate) if the change exceeds a 10% variance from the baseline.

ARTICLE II: THE CITIZENSHIP TIERS
Citizenship is not a right of birth, but a Proof of Capability. The society is stratified into functional castes to ensure economic efficiency.
SECTION 1: THE NOBILITY (TIERS 1-7)
•	Definition: Citizens who own a portion of the finite RGB space.
•	Rights:
o	Right to Collect Rent.
o	Right to Vote in Local City Elections.
o	Right to Mint Sub-Tokens (Items) within their territory.
•	Duties:
o	The Activity Tax: Landowners must cryptographically sign a "Proof of Life" periodically. Failure to maintain the land results in Entropy (Decay) and eventual foreclosure.
SECTION 2: THE COMMONS (TIERS 8-14)
•	Definition: Citizens who provide labor, liquidity, and creativity.
•	Rights:
o	Right to Earn and Trade $CLR.
o	Right to Fair Trial (via the Monoliths).
o	Right to Privacy (Data Sovereignty).
•	Mobility: Any Commoner may ascend to Nobility by acquiring Land on the open market. There are no caste barriers based on race, religion, or real-world nationality; only economic merit.

ARTICLE III: THE JUDICIARY (THE ORDER OF MONOLITHS)
Justice in the Digital Nation is not dispensed by algorithms alone, nor by a central moderator, but by a decentralized peer network of high-reputation citizens known as The Monoliths (Tier 2).
SECTION 1: JURISDICTION AND SCOPE
The Judiciary holds authority over:
1.	Civil Disputes: Disagreements regarding smart contracts (e.g., "I paid for a texture pack but received the wrong file").
2.	Identity Verification: The validation of "Proof of Personhood" documents for new citizens (Immigration Control).
3.	Code of Conduct: Violations of community standards (Harrassment, Hate Speech, Griefing).
Note: Technical Exploits (Hacking the server) are not subject to trial; they are subject to immediate protocol-level banishment by the Imperial Guard.
SECTION 2: THE TRIBUNAL PROTOCOL
To ensure impartiality, the judicial process utilizes a Blind Sharding Mechanism.
•	Clause A (Random Selection): When a dispute is filed, the System selects 5 Monoliths via a Verifiable Random Function (VRF). These judges are anonymous to each other and to the disputing parties.
•	Clause B (The Blind Ballot): Judges review the evidence (Chat Logs, Transaction Hashes) independently. They must cast a vote (UPHOLD or DISMISS) without seeing the votes of their peers.
•	Clause C (The Schelling Point Incentive):
o	Judges who vote with the Majority receive a stipend in $CLR (funded by court fees).
o	Judges who vote with the Minority (against the consensus) receive nothing and suffer a penalty to their Reputation Score.
o	Logic: This Game Theory mechanism forces judges to seek the objective truth rather than accepting bribes, as they cannot coordinate a false verdict with unknown peers.
SECTION 3: THE HIGH COURT AND APPEALS
The decision of the Monoliths is generally final. However, in cases involving amounts greater than 100,000 $CLR, an appeal may be lodged.
•	The Council Appeal: The case is escalated to a sub-committee of the Genesis Council (Tier 1).
•	Cost of Appeal: The appellant must stake a significant bond. If the appeal is deemed frivolous, the bond is burned.
SECTION 4: INTEGRITY AND REMOVAL
A Monolith is a lifetime appointment, conditional on behavior.
•	The Watchmen Protocol: The System algorithmically monitors the voting patterns of Monoliths. A Monolith who deviates statistically from the consensus (e.g., voting "Innocent" on obvious spam cases) is flagged for audit.
•	Impeachment: Upon confirmation of malice or incompetence, the Monolith is stripped of Tier 2 status and their staked assets are seized by the Treasury.

ARTICLE IV: THE FISCAL COMPACT (THE ECONOMY)
The Nation requires resources to maintain the servers and defend the code.
SECTION 1: THE IMPERIAL TAX (THE 0.05%)
A non-negotiable protocol fee of 0.05% is levied on every value transfer within the network. This funds the "Dev Ops" (Server costs, Engineer salaries) and the "Reserve Fund" (Market stability).
SECTION 2: LOCAL FISCAL SOVEREIGNTY
Tier 3 Architects (Mayors) possess the sovereign right to levy local taxes on commercial activity within their Hex-Instance.
•	Limit: No local tax may exceed 20%.
•	Competition: Citizens are free to migrate their assets to low-tax jurisdictions (Tiebout Model). The Empire guarantees the right of exit.

ARTICLE V: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND COMMERCE
The Nation recognizes that the fruit of the mind is the ultimate property.
SECTION 1: THE CREATOR'S RIGHT (TIER 11 SOVEREIGNTY)
Unlike legacy platforms where user-generated content belongs to the corporation, ColorIsLive establishes absolute creator ownership.
•	The Blueprint Protocol: When a Tier 11 Crafter mints a design (e.g., a 3D sneaker, a texture pack, a script), the Intellectual Property (IP) rights are cryptographically bound to their wallet.
•	Royalties: The Right to Royalty is inalienable. The System automatically enforces a creator fee (default 5%) on all secondary market sales of that item, in perpetuity. No future update can remove this right.
SECTION 2: BRAND PROTECTION (REAL-WORLD INTEGRATION)
To attract external capital ("Terra Firma" entities like Nike, Gucci, or Artists), the Nation enforces strict anti-counterfeiting laws.
•	The Verification Mark: Real-world entities may apply for "Verified Status."
•	The Ban on Clones: It is a high crime to mint a digital asset that infringes upon a registered trademark. The Monoliths (Tier 2) are empowered to issue "Takedown Orders" (burning the offending assets) upon valid evidence of infringement. This guarantees a safe harbor for global brands.

ARTICLE VI: EXTERNAL RELATIONS AND COMPLIANCE
The Digital Nation exists within the physical infrastructure of Earth. We are sovereign, but not isolated.
SECTION 1: THE BRIDGE TO TERRA FIRMA
The Nation maintains a diplomatic stance of "Compliance without Subservience."
•	KYC/AML: To protect the economy from becoming a haven for money laundering, the "Bureau of Immigration" (Tier 2) enforces strict Know-Your-Customer (KYC) protocols at the fiat-gateway points.
•	Tax Reporting: The System provides tools for citizens to export their transaction history for compliance with their physical tax jurisdictions (IRS, etc.). We facilitate compliance; we do not enforce it for foreign powers.
SECTION 2: NEUTRALITY
ColorIsLive is a non-aligned digital territory. We do not participate in the geopolitical conflicts of the physical world. A citizen's rank is determined solely by their contribution to the Code, never by their nationality, religion, or political affiliation on Earth.
